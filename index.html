About Me

Hello, thanks for stopping by! My name is Kyla, a Narrative Designer passionate about storytelling in interactive media. When I'm not designing narratives, I love mentoring future engineers through FIRST Robotics.

My Journey: From my early days designing board games with my father, to creating worlds through narrative design, I've always been driven by my love of gaming and storytelling. I now focus on bringing immersive and engaging narratives to life in interactive media.

Echoes of the Salt Flats

Echoes of the Salt Flats is a Tabletop RPG featuring a unique crafting mechanic, designed to explore how worldbuilding surfaces in different forms of interactive media.

Fluxians have been living peacefully with their Terror Birds for years. But when Terror Birds suddenly start passing away on the borders of the salt flats, it is up to the crew to figure out whyâ€”and how to stop their sacred companions from being killed.

Narrative Designer: Throughout this year-long project, I worked closely with Colin Gallagher to create a unique TTRPG. I took on many roles during this project, including social media manager, showcase manager, and narrative designer. All artwork was created by Saida Bahtierova.

Plates of Power

Plates of Power is a rogue-like game inspired by Latino culture, emphasizing power, failure, and the importance of community. Returning home from college, Ale Zardoya hears of her sister's disappearance and must embrace the role of Champion as she embarks on a mission to find her.

Social Media Manager: I was the sole Social Media Marketing Manager for Plates of Power. During the 14 weeks with this team, I created a social media campaign to engage the community and promote the game.

To view my work, please visit the Linktree for Plates of Power's home studio, Studio Berto.

Celestial Guardians

Celestial Guardians is a strategy game set in a world where celestial beings must defend their elements. Players must choose wisely to restore balance to the world before it's too late.

Key Features:

  • Turn-based tactical gameplay
  • Elemental guardians with unique abilities
  • Strategic resource management
