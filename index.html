About Me

Hello, thanks for stopping by! My name is Kyla, a Narrative Designer passionate about storytelling in interactive media. When I'm not designing narratives, I love mentoring future engineers through FIRST Robotics.

My Journey: From my early days designing board games with my father, to creating worlds through narrative design, I've always been driven by my love of gaming and storytelling. I now focus on bringing immersive and engaging narratives to life in interactive media.

Feel free to reach out to me via email or connect with me on LinkedIn!

Plates of Power

Plates of Power is a rogue-like game inspired by Latino Culture, emphasizing power, failure, and the importance of community.

Returning home from college, Ale Zardoya hears of her sister's disappearance and must embrace the role of Champion

as she embarks on a mission to find her.

Key Features:

  • Action-based combat system
  • Branching storylines
  • Mythological lore integration

Celestial Guardians

A strategy game set in a world where celestial beings must defend their elements. Players must choose wisely to restore balance to the world before it's too late.

Key Features:

  • Turn-based tactical gameplay
  • Elemental guardians with unique abilities
  • Strategic resource management
