About Me

Thanks for stopping by! My name is Kyla, and I am a passionate Narrative Designer. In my free time, I mentor and serve as an alumni of FIRST, where you can often find me in the judge's room at local FIRST LEGO League (FLL) and FIRST Robotics Competitions (FRC), providing valuable feedback to young engineers.

About my journey: Ever since I was a young girl, I have enjoyed gaming. Growing up, my family would play tabletop games with our meals, teaching us kids critical thinking skills from an early age. However, my passion for gaming did not stop there. As I grew older, my father and I would create board games together, creating unique game mechanics based off of our most beloved games.
